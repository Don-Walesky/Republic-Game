# Wave 05: Economy

**How decisions ripple through economics.**

**Duration**: 4-6 weeks

**Status**: 🔴 Not Started

**Depends On**: Wave 4 (Government decisions) + Wave 2 (World economy)

**Milestone Gate**: "Your economy responds to your rule"

---

## Objective

Build economic systems. Player sees their policies ripple through production, trade, jobs, and prices.

By the end of Wave 5:
- Production responding to policy
- Trade changing based on decisions
- Jobs created or lost
- Prices fluctuating
- Revenue flowing based on taxes
- Economic crises from poor policy
- Ready for Wave 6 (legislature)

---

## Systems to Build

### 1. Production System
- Production facilities
- Industry types
- Production capacity
- Production efficiency
- Production costs

### 2. Trade System
- Import/export mechanics
- Trade routes
- Trade agreements
- Trade partners
- Trade balance

### 3. Market System
- Market prices
- Supply/demand dynamics
- Price fluctuation
- Market crashes
- Market booms

### 4. Labor System
- Employment rates
- Wages
- Job creation
- Job loss
- Labor unions

### 5. Taxation System
- Tax rates
- Tax types (income, corporate, etc)
- Tax revenue
- Tax compliance
- Tax evasion

### 6. Business System
- Companies and industries
- Business profitability
- Business failure
- Business growth
- Market competition

### 7. Income Distribution
- Wealth gap
- Inequality metrics
- Class tensions
- Poverty levels
- Wealth growth

### 8. Economic Advisors
- Economist recommendations
- Economic forecasts
- Crisis warnings
- Opportunity suggestions
- Economic analysis

### 9. Economic Crises
- Recessions
- Depressions
- Inflation crises
- Unemployment crises
- Financial crises

### 10. Trade Routes
- Trade route generation
- Trade route profitability
- Trade route vulnerability
- Trade route expansion
- Trade piracy
