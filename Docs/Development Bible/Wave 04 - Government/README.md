# Wave 04: Government

**How the player wields power.**

**Duration**: 6-8 weeks

**Status**: 🔴 Not Started

**Depends On**: Wave 3 (Campaign) - player won the election

**Milestone Gate**: "You have the power to govern"

---

## Objective

Build the government system. The player has won and now must govern.

By the end of Wave 4:
- Cabinet formed with ministers
- Ministers propose policies
- Player makes decisions
- Decisions affect the world
- Approval rating tracks performance
- Crises emerge and must be handled
- Ready for Wave 5 (economy)

---

## Systems to Build

### 1. Cabinet System
- Minister roles
- Minister hiring/firing
- Minister specialization
- Minister loyalty
- Minister competence

### 2. Executive Powers
- What the player can decide
- Power limitations
- Constitutional constraints
- Emergency powers
- Veto powers

### 3. Government Interface
- Cabinet room
- Decision Spaces
- Minister panels
- Report viewing
- Archive access

### 4. Advisory System
- Ministers propose policies
- Policy proposals with consequences
- Minister reasoning
- Multiple options per decision
- Minister disagreement

### 5. Decision System
- Decision lifecycle
- Option evaluation
- Consequence preview
- Decision making
- Decision consequences

### 6. Approval System
- Approval tracking
- Approval changes
- Public opinion by region
- Approval history
- Approval thresholds

### 7. Crisis System
- Crisis generation
- Crisis severity
- Crisis resolution options
- Crisis consequences
- Multiple crises

### 8. Legitimacy System
- Power mandate tracking
- Legitimacy from approval
- Legitimacy from constitutional rules
- Loss of legitimacy threshold
- Coup possibility

### 9. Minister Specialization
- Finance minister
- Defense minister
- Social minister
- Foreign minister
- Interior minister
- Other specialties

### 10. Resource Allocation
- Budget allocation
- Spending priorities
- Resource competition
- Budget constraints
- Year-over-year changes
