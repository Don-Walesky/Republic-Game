# The Republic Manifesto

**Republic is not a game about ruling people.**

**It is a game about carrying the burden of democratic leadership.**

---

This single statement is the north star of Republic.

Whenever we debate a new mechanic, whenever we question a design decision, whenever we face a choice between approaches:

**Ask: Does this make the player feel the burden, responsibility, and complexity of leadership?**

If the answer is yes, it belongs in Republic.

If not, we rethink it.

That's our design compass. Everything else flows from this.
